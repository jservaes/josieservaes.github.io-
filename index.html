Projects

Payroll Automation

A Python-based software I developed for ClearRock to streamline the payroll process. It pulls time entries from QuickBooks Time via API, matches them with employee pay rates, and generates payroll-ready reports for each pay period. I also created a custom GUI to allow anyone in the company to run payroll with no technical knowledge, enhancing reporting accuracy and reducing manual effort..

Tech Stack: Python, Flask, REST APIs, Excel

Database Cleaner

A data-cleaning and structuring project for Elder Resource Benefits Consulting. I audited and mapped their Salesforce databases, eliminating redundancy and reformatting data structures to align with form automation in Conga. This cleanup enabled more reliable form generation and simplified future data entry.

Tech Stack: Salesforce, Excel, Conga

Automating Quality Control

A Python-based software I developed for a client company to streamline their quality control process. The tool scrapes customer reviews from multiple websites and internal documents, then processes the data into structured categorical fields for the company’s quality control team to review.
